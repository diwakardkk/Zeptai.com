# 🏥 What is a Clinical Summary?

A clinical summary is a short, structured report about a patient.

It usually includes:

- Main complaint  
- Symptoms  
- Duration  
- Medical history  
- Medications  
- Observations  

👉 Doctors use this to understand the patient quickly.

Instead of asking everything from scratch, they get a **ready overview**.
