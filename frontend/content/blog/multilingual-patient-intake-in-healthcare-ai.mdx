Multilingual intake is one of the clearest opportunities for practical healthcare AI. Patients often describe symptoms in their most comfortable language, and health systems work better when that language can be preserved rather than flattened too early.

WHO's digital health work consistently emphasizes equity and access. Language access is part of that. A multilingual intake layer can support clearer patient narratives, reduce misunderstanding, and improve the quality of the first clinical handoff.
