2. Nature of Services

The platform provides an advanced AI-powered clinical documentation and pre-screening assistant designed to support healthcare professionals in capturing, organizing, and reviewing patient information in a structured format.

The system operates through a guided, voice-based interaction model, enabling patients to communicate their symptoms and medical concerns naturally.


Operational Workflow

  • The AI assistant interacts with patients via a voice interface 
  • Patients provide health-related information including symptoms and concerns 
  • The system processes the interaction in real time using AI technologies 
  • The conversation is converted into structured clinical summaries 
  • These summaries are made available to healthcare professionals through integrated systems or dashboards

The platform is designed to:

  • Enhance consultation efficiency

All clinical decisions remain the sole responsibility of qualified healthcare professionals.
